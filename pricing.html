❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Still have questions? These are the ones we hear most often — answered honestly.

Do you require contracts?

Setup projects are a one-time engagement — no long-term contract required. Monthly retainer plans are month-to-month. We earn your continued business by doing good work, not by locking you in.

How long does setup take?

Most Starter builds go live within 5–10 business days. Growth and Enterprise projects typically take 3–6 weeks depending on complexity, integrations, and how quickly we receive your business info. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

What if I need changes after launch?

Minor tweaks in the first 30 days are included at no charge. After that, changes are covered under a monthly retainer plan or billed at our standard hourly rate. We'll always be transparent about what a change will cost before we touch anything.

Do you offer refunds?

We stand behind our work. If we haven't started building yet, we'll refund your deposit in full. Once work is underway, we'll work with you to make it right before we talk about refunds. We're not interested in taking your money for something that doesn't work for you.
